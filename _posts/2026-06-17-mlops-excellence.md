## Beyond the Notebook
Many AI projects fail because they never leave the Jupyter Notebook. MLOps is the bridge that brings research into production with stability and reproducibility.

## Version Control for Everything
In traditional software, we version code. In ML, we must version code, data, and models. Tools like DVC (Data Version Control) are vital for maintaining a single source of truth.

## Automated Pipelines
A robust ML product requires automated training and deployment pipelines. This includes data validation, model testing (evaluating accuracy and bias), and automated canary deployments.

## The Cultural Shift
Successful MLOps requires collaboration between Data Scientists and Engineers. It's about bringing the rigor of software testing and CI/CD to the experimental world of Machine Learning.
