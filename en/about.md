Hi! I'm Sana.

I am a Software Engineer by trade, specializing in Backend Engineering and System Design. I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence to deepen my expertise in practical machine learning systems.

Most of my recent work sits at the intersection of robust backend development and applied AI - building scalable web architectures and deploying production-ready Computer Vision pipelines.

Occasionally, I find time to:

* Optimize local inference for open-source Large Language Models under tight hardware constraints.
* Build deep learning-based vehicle analysis applications, such as [car-inspect-ai](https://github.com/sanayasfp/car-inspect-ai), designed for damage evaluation and automated physical KYC.
* Develop scalable, location-based TypeScript services with high-performance architectures like [laplace-nearby](https://github.com/sanayasfp/laplace-nearby).
* Study modern computer system architectures, MLOps lifecycles, and high-performance computing (HPC) software optimization.

In my free time, I love recreating dishes I see on social media. It’s my favorite way to unplug from screens, fuel my creativity, and share great food. I also enjoy exploring new tech stacks and reading systems engineering blogs, my favorite one being [ByteByteGo](https://substack.com/@bytebytego399569).
